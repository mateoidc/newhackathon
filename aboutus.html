About Us

"One day in 2009, a friend who I loved dearly passed away due to the lack of proper healthcare; motivating me to want to make a change" Says One of our head Research Analyzers. In 2011 after research for a biology project a small group of classmates decided to further their research to create a solution for the minorities in our community who could not afford proper health care insurance such as Signa, Blue Cross + Blue Shield and Medicare unknowing that it would have such an impact later down the line. It started with many trials over a course of 5 years our team finally made a break and were able to create a prototype and begun testing in 2016 which was tested for 2 years on many different age groups, health conditions & biological backgrounds helping us to find what was affective & safe we realized in 2018 that the original tablet was in fact harmful to children's brain growth causing us to reach out to multiple pediatric resources for medical help including monitored trials and medical study on the brain development of young persons resulting in us making a tablet specifically beneficial for people under the age of 17, an age when the brain is most active in its development. In 2019 we begun testing TreasureTablet youth™ and within weeks we got successful feedback and began manufacturing pills & bottling them immediately. As of march 2020 TreasureTablet™ & TreasureTablet youth™ is FDA approved, all natural & organic.
